My Tips

01

Beginnings are always difficult

When you start doing something new, whatever it is, you will feel that it is difficult at first, but continue a little bit.

وَأَن لَّيْسَ لِلْإِنسَانِ إِلَّا مَا سَعَىٰ
02

Trust in Allah and then in yourself

This is something that cannot be learned, only acquired.

وَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ ۚ وَكَفَىٰ بِاللَّهِ وَكِيلًا
03

Your friends = you

You are not obligated to anyone. You must always choose the right friend. I do not support being friendless, but you must choose a right friend. You can always determine relationships through your dealings with them. Be humble. Do not cut off your relationship with anyone. Be friendly with everyone, because you may need them at some point.

وَقُولُوا لِلنَّاسِ حُسْنًا
04

Your parents

You will regret this later. Fix things with your family and live up to their expectations. They are your path to heaven and they didn't see all this in vain, did they? No matter what they did, they were right.

وَاخْفِضْ لَهُمَا جَنَاحَ الذُّلِّ مِنَ الرَّحْمَةِ وَقُلْ رَبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا
05

Instant pleasure

Don't be a fool, what starts fast ends fast

إِنَّ اللَّـهَ كَانَ عَلَيْكُمْ رَقِيبًا
